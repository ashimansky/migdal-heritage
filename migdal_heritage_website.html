The Holy Land

Magdala.
A Place of Living History.

On the western shore of the Sea of Galilee, in the land where Jesus walked and preached, lies Magdala — birthplace of Mary Magdalene.

Mary was healed by Jesus, stood faithfully at the foot of the Cross when others fled, and was chosen — above all the disciples — to be the first witness of the Resurrection. Her hometown carries the weight of that sacred history to this day.

"Mary!" — Jesus spoke her name, and she knew.

Understanding Your Guardianship

What a Guardianship Is — and What It Isn't

Clarity and transparency are foundational to this project. Please read this carefully before you acquire a Guardianship.

A Guardianship IS

✓

A Licensed Custodial Certificate linking you irrevocably to a specific, verified 1 sq cm coordinate within the Magdala heritage site

✓

A lifetime spiritual guardianship — a permanent commemorative right, not a transactional purchase

✓

A premium physical artefact — a laser-etched K9 crystal block and gold-foil certificate, heirloom quality

✓

A digitally verifiable claim — your serial number is searchable on the Magdala Global Registry

✓

A contribution to charitable works in your community — 10% of your purchase goes directly to the Magdala Faithful Foundation

vs

A Guardianship is NOT

✗

A real estate title deed or conveyance of any property rights under Israeli, Kenyan, or any other law

✗

An investment or financial instrument — it carries no promise of capital appreciation or financial return

✗

A collective investment scheme — you are not purchasing a share in any fund, company, or land-buying enterprise

✗

A charitable donation — you are acquiring a tangible product with a named commemorative value

✗

A right of physical access — guardianship does not entitle the holder to enter or use the land

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I own a piece of land in Israel?

+

No. A Guardianship is a Symbolic Commemorative Custodianship — not a property title or real estate transaction. You acquire a licensed custodial certificate linking you to a specific coordinate on the Magdala heritage site. No freehold or leasehold real estate title is transferred. The land itself is held by the Magdala Faithful Foundation in perpetual heritage custodianship.

What is my serial number and why does it matter?

+

Your serial number — in the format MIG-XXXXXXXX — is your unique, permanent identifier in the Magdala Global Registry. It links you specifically to a 1 sq cm coordinate within the heritage site and is laser-engraved on your crystal block. No two serial numbers are the same. You can verify your Guardianship at any time on this website by entering your serial number above.

Is this an investment? Can I sell my Guardianship?

+

No. A Guardianship carries no promise of financial return, capital appreciation, or resale market. It is a devotional and commemorative product, not a financial instrument. It is not regulated by the Capital Markets Authority of Kenya or any securities regulator. We explicitly advise against purchasing a Guardianship with any expectation of financial gain.

How does my purchase benefit the community?

+

10% of every purchase is automatically routed to the Magdala Faithful Foundation at the point of sale via the M-Pesa Daraja API — the commercial entity never handles this portion. The Foundation, governed by independent trustees including respected clergy and community leaders, disburses these funds to charitable projects including clean water, education, and community development. Disbursement reports are published quarterly.

How long does delivery take?

+

Your Certificate of Guardianship is issued immediately upon payment — either physically at the point of church sale, or by email if purchased online. Your Cornerstone Crystal Block is laser-engraved at our Nairobi facility and delivered within 28 days. Church purchasers receive their crystal block at a community Redemption Sunday event. Online purchasers receive theirs by secure courier.

Can I purchase a Guardianship as a gift?

+

Yes — and this is one of the most common uses. A Guardianship Kit makes a deeply meaningful gift for weddings, confirmations, baptisms, birthdays, and memorials. Gift purchases can be registered in the recipient's name. Contact us for gift certificate packaging options.

Which churches currently participate?

+

We are currently onboarding church partners across Kenya including Mega-denominations, Catholic Dioceses, Anglican Synods, and Christian SACCOs. Our launch programme prioritises Nairobi, Mombasa, Nakuru, Eldoret, and Kisumu. If your church or denomination would like to become a Guardianship Ambassador Institution, please contact us using the details below.
